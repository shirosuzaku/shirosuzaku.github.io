Association of Ethiopian Architects

AEA

The Association of Ethiopian Architects (AEA) was founded on August 1991 G.C. in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, with the objective to promote the advancement of architecture in the country, conduct CPD trainings and research works, ensure members adhere to professional code of ethics in the practice and generally keep professional interests of its members. Furthermore, it strives to bridge gaps among professionals working in the built environment, the private sector, governmental institutions and non-governmental institutions.

Mission

To engage proactively and compressively for the advancement of Architecture through design excellence and an ethical approach to the built environment

Vision

To be a recognized, leading institution in shaping the built environment in the region by 2038.

Why Collaborate with us?

Over the last 31 years of its existence, AEA has become a prominent body of professionals that is making strides in having positive influences to make our professionals in the built environment create environmentally, socially and economically conscious spaces. In addition to that AEA; is also an accredited member of two international professional organizations representing architects of the world, named the Union of International Architects (UIA) and Africa Union of Architects (AUA)
